LEGO

"LEGO" is an abbreviation of the two Danish words "leg godt" meaning "play well!"

The LEGO Company is a Danish toy production company based in Billund. It manufactures Lego-brand toys, consisting mostly of interlocking plastic bricks. The Lego Group has also built several amusement parks around the world, each known as Legoland, and operates numerous retail stores. To find out more about this company visit their official website.
